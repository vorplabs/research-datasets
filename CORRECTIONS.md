# Corrections

Vorp Labs Research welcomes specific, source-backed corrections.

Submit a correction through the [Vorp Labs contact form](https://vorplabs.com/contact) with:

1. Dataset name, version, and record anchor or row.
2. The field believed to be wrong or stale.
3. A primary-source URL and the relevant effective or publication date.
4. A concise explanation of the proposed correction.

Accepted corrections are released as a new version when they change an immutable snapshot. Prior releases remain available so citations can be reproduced.
